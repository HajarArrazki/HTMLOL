We are a team of three students from the University of Bristol who were tired of the struggle of finding a movie to watch that we all like. From this, the idea was born of a digital interface that would pick out the movie for you. Playing with the 'Netflix and Chill' concept, the website was to pair you with a like-minded movie-goer, which has now expanded beyond dates to friend groups and get-togethers.
